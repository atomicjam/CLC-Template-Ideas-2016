Jobs

Cheltenham Ladies' College is a very special place to work, employing approximately 600 staff in both teaching and non-teaching staff roles.

The main College site is situated in the centre of the beautiful Regency town of Cheltenham, with our boarding houses and sports facilities a short walk away.

As a teacher here you'll enjoy teaching motivated, intelligent girls who want to learn and you'll have access to great facilities to support teaching and learning.

Non-teaching roles at College are diverse, from Chef to Web Developer, Gardener to HR Officer. Whatever your role, you'll benefit from being part of a large team with opportunities for development and, in many cases, the ability to work flexible hours.

Benefits

We are genuinely committed to our staff and endeavour to offer a range of benefits that people will value and enjoy. These include:

  • a contributory pension scheme;
  • salary sacrifice options;
  • membership of our Sports Centre available at minimal cost for yourself and at a reduced rate for your family - facilities include a pool, fitness suite, sports hall and squash courts;
  • employee helpline;
  • flexible hours - dependent on role;
  • subsidised restaurant;
  • training and development opportunities.
