Key Information

Pupils

There are around 850 girls in College, spread across Lower College, Upper College and Sixth Form. Approximately 75% are boarders and 25% day girls. Around 150 new girls join us each year, mainly in Lower College (Years 7-9) and Sixth Form (Year 12). Girls come from all over Gloucestershire, the UK and the world. Many have never boarded before joining us and they have come from over 400 different junior schools and diverse educational backgrounds.

Staff

The current full-time teaching staff comprises 89 women and 35 men. There are a further 39 part-time teachers and over 40 peripatetic music and drama teachers. More than 85 of the teachers act as tutors to the girls during their time in College. There are 15 Housemistresses, each with their own team of house staff, to care for the girls.

College has an excellent team of IT, administrative, catering, domestic and works support staff. They all work hard to ensure that our organisation, services and provisions are second to none.
